Traditional Event Schedule

🪔
Pocham Panduga
April 19 · Evening
A floral festival honouring village goddess Pochamma—women light oil lamps, sing, and decorate with Bathukamma patterns.
🌼
Haldi (Pasupu) Ceremony
April 20 · Morning
Turmeric anointment for the bride & groom, with folk songs, joyful laughter, and saffron scents in the air!
👑
Pelli Koduku
April 21 · Evening
Groom’s grand welcome procession, garland exchange, and blessings exchanged at the mandapam entrance.
🔥
Jilakara Bellam
April 23 · Muhurtham (10:16 AM)
At the auspicious moment, cumin and jaggery placed on each other's heads—cherishing strength and sweetness for all time.
💫
Mudu Mullu
After Muhurtham
Three sacred knots tied to the bride’s Mangalyam, representing body, mind, and soul—Telugu tradition’s heart.
💒
Kalyanam (Marriage Ceremony)
April 23 · Forenoon
Saptapadi vows around the fire, joyful ululations, jasmine garlands, and blessings from both families as two hearts unite.
