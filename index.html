Celebration of the Sacred Rituals
🪔

Pocham Panduga

April 19 · Evening
The Auspicious Beginning

An ancient ceremony honouring Pochamma Thalli, the revered village deity. The womenfolk gather in celebration, lighting sacred clay lamps and adorning spaces with resplendent floral rangoli and bathukamma flowers—invoking divine blessings with melodious folk traditions.
🌼

Haldi Ceremony

April 20 · Morning
The Golden Blessing

The sacred turmeric ritual where golden blessings are lovingly applied. Enveloped in ancestral grace, joy, and the laughter of beloved family, this timeless ceremony invokes radiance, health, and eternal prosperity for our new life together.
👑

Pelli Koduku

April 21 · Evening
The Groom's Sacred Anointing

A vibrant Telangana tradition of great significance. Close kinfolk gather to bestow sacred blessings as the groom is adorned in festive raiment and readied for the wedding. Music, mirth, and ancestral benedictions fill the air, celebrating this momentous evening.
🔥

Jilakara Bellam & Mudu Mullu

April 23 · 10:16 AM – 10:18 AM
The Sacred Union

The most hallowed moment before the divine fire. Cumin and jaggery exchange, symbols of an eternal, sweet marital bond. Three sacred knots bind the mangalyam—each representing body, mind, soul, and unity through all seasons of life.
