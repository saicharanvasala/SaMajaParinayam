Pocham Panduga
April 19 · Evening
The auspicious beginning: An offering to Pochamma Thalli, the village deity. Womenfolk gather for folk songs, light clay lamps, and decorate spaces with bathukamma and beautiful floral rangoli, blessing the wedding journey ahead.
🌼
Haldi (Pasupu) Ceremony
April 20 · Morning
The golden touch: Turmeric is gently applied to the bride and groom, enveloping them in blessings, joy, and the laughter of family—invoking auspiciousness and radiant health for their new life.
👑
Pelli Koduku
April 21 · Evening
The groom’s anointing: A vibrant traditional Telangana ritual. Close relatives gather for a sacred blessing, and the groom is readied for the wedding, adorned in festive attire and greeted with music and mirth.
🔥
Jilakara Bellam
April 23 · Muhurtham: 10:16 AM
The sacred moment: The bride and groom each place cumin and jaggery on one another, symbolizing an unbreakable and sweet marital bond before the sacred fire. As vows are exchanged:
  • Mudu Mullu: Three knots are tied to the mangalyam, each representing a promise to join together in body, mind, and soul—the heart of Telugu wedding tradition.
